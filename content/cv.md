Current Affiliation
-------------------

### Postdoctoral research associate

Washington University in St. Louis

I am investigating changes in the strength in the Pacific Walker Circulation
during the past 1000 years, using a recently compiled
<a href="https://essd.copernicus.org/articles/12/2261/2020/" target="_blank">database</a>
of water isotope proxy records. I also use existing measurements of the stable isotopic composition of
precipitation to investigate drivers of spatial and temporal variability
in the global water cycle.
